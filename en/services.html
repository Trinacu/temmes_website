Services

What We Offer

Full support along the entire commercial chain

We support our clients not only in trading operations, but in all the operational and regulatory aspects that an international transaction entails.

Transport

Multimodal logistics

We coordinate transport by road, rail, sea, and air, selecting the most efficient combination based on the nature of the goods, lead times, and costs. We manage the entire logistics chain from origin to final delivery, with reliable partners across all regions.

Certification

Dangerous goods and certification

We handle the preparation and verification of all documentation required for dangerous goods: safety data sheets, ADR, IMDG, and IATA certificates, and country-specific authorisations. Regulatory compliance is an integral part of our service, not an add-on.

Regulation

Legislation in operating countries

Every country has its own rules on import/export, duties, restrictions, and authorisations. Our team monitors regulations across the markets we operate in and ensures every transaction meets current legal requirements, reducing risk and delays.

Languages

Translation and language support

We provide language support for contracts, technical documentation, and commercial communications. We operate in Italian, English, Russian, and the main Eastern European languages, facilitating communication between parties at every stage of a transaction.
