Who We Are

A company built on relationships

Temmes was founded in the early 2000s with a clear purpose: to facilitate trade between the markets of Eastern and Western Europe. As these two worlds were drawing closer together, we built the first networks of contacts with producers and buyers, learning the specifics of each market through direct experience.

Over the years we expanded our presence across the food, chemical, and industrial raw materials sectors. Today we operate in over twenty countries, with an approach that combines commercial experience, technical knowledge, and a network of relationships built over time.

Our strength is not our size, but the depth of the relationships we have cultivated.
