Where we operate

Temmes' commercial network spans strategic markets across four geographic areas, with established relationships and direct presence in key trading centres.

Italy, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, and central and eastern European countries. Our primary reference market.

Middle East

UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and Gulf markets. A high-growth area for both volume and frequency of trade.

Asia

China, India, Vietnam, and Southeast Asian markets. Established presence across the manufacturing supply chain.

North Africa

Morocco, Tunisia, Egypt, and Algeria. Emerging markets with growing demand for industrial and consumer products.
